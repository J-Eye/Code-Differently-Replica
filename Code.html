Together we can move the needle of diversity in tech.

Code Differently provides hands on training and education through coding classes that gives participants the technical and cognitive skills they need to excel in technology-driven workplaces

Our Programs

1000 Kids Coding

The Code Differently 1000 Kids Coding program was created to
expose New Castle County students to computing and
programming. The 1000 Kids Coding courses are designed for all
experience levels, no experience required.

Pipeline DevShops

Pipeline DevShop is a youth work-based learning program. Youth
participants experience working in a real software development
environment while sharpening their technology and soft skills.

Why Code Differently?

75% of our high school participants will continue their education or career in technology related fields. 95% of our college particpants recieved offers for internships or full-time employment in technology organizations. 96% of our program participants identity as ethnically underrespresented. 41% of our participants are women.

Our Apprach

We designed each program on the foundation of five strategic pillars:

Attraction:
We employ innovative initiatives to spark participants' curiosity about the tech industry.

Exposure
We expose participant to the amazing world of technology and coding.

Engagement
We keep participants engaged by fostering more in-depth learning of principles and concepts.

Ownership
We help participants develop a sense of ownership of their new skill set by providing opportunities for them to develop real technology solutions.

Empowerment
We empower participants to inspire others to explore tech careers.

Strengthening the technology ecosystem by creating mutually beneficial relationships between communities and corporate partners.
